Grilled Chicken Salad Recipe

Description

We will be making grilled chicken salad in this recipe. This will be high in protein and low in fat. Enjoy the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 3 cups mixed greens (e.g., romaine, spinach, arugula)
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ cucumber, sliced
  • ¼ red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp plain non-fat Greek yogurt (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep the Chicken
    • Pat the chicken breast dry. Rub with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill the Chicken
    • Preheat grill or grill pan over medium heat. Lightly spray with cooking spray (optional).
    • Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). Let rest 2-3 minutes, then slice thinly.
  3. Assemble the Salad
    • In a large bowl, toss the greens, tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
  4. Dress It Up
    • Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, or mix Greek yogurt with vinegar for a creamy, low-fat dressing.
  5. Top and Serve
    • Lay sliced grilled chicken over the salad. Serve immediately.

Nutritional Info (per serving)

  • Calories: 280 cal
  • Protein: 35g
  • Fat: 9g
  • Carbs: 10g
