Protein-Packed Smoothie Recipe

Description

In this recipe we will learn how to make a protein smoothie. This smoothie will have a great amount of protein and is low in fat. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds or flaxseeds
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients: Add all ingredients (protein powder, almond milk, banana, spinach, chia seeds, Greek yogurt, and ice cubes) into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth: Pulse the blender and blend on high for about 30-60 seconds, or until the mixture is smooth and creamy. If it's too thick, add a bit more almond milk or water to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Taste and adjust: If you prefer a sweeter smoothie, feel free to add a few extra slices of banana or a small splash of honey or stevia. Blend again if needed.
  4. Serve immediately: Pour into a glass and enjoy! You can also take it in a shaker bottle for a portable snack or meal replacement.

Nutritional Info (per serving):

  • Calories: ~250-300
  • Protein: ~35-30g
  • Fat: ~7-9g
  • Carbs: ~20-25g
