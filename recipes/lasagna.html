Lasagna Recipe

Description

In this recipe we will learn how to make lasagna. This lasagna will have a huge amount of protein and less fat than your traditional lasagna. Let's get started!

Ingredients

    For the noodles:

  • 9 whole-wheat lasagna noodles

    • Protein-packed filling:

  • 1 lb (450g) lean ground turkey or chicken breast
  • 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the marinara sauce:

  • 2 cups low-sodium marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped (optional)

    • For the top layer:

  • 1 cup part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated (optional, for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Cook the noodles: Follow the package instructions for the lasagna noodles. If using zucchini slices, slice them thinly lengthwise using a mandolin or sharp knife. You don’t need to cook them; they’ll soften as the lasagna bakes.
  3. Prepare the filling:
    • In a large skillet, heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add the ground turkey or chicken and cook, breaking it up with a spoon until browned and fully cooked (about 7-10 minutes). Drain any excess liquid or fat.
    • Add the onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the onions are soft.
    • Remove from heat and allow the mixture to cool slightly. Once cooled, mix in the spinach (if using), cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, and egg (or egg whites). Stir everything until well combined.
  4. Make the sauce: In a separate saucepan, combine the marinara sauce, tomato paste, red pepper flakes, and fresh basil (if using). Stir together and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes on low heat.
  5. Assemble the lasagna:
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the marinara sauce on the bottom.
    • Add a layer of noodles (or zucchini slices) on top.
    • Spread half of the protein filling mixture over the noodles.
    • Add another layer of sauce, then another layer of noodles (or zucchini).
    • Spread the remaining protein filling over the second layer.
    • Top with the final layer of noodles and finish with the remaining sauce.
  6. Add the cheese: Sprinkle the top with the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese (optional, for flavor).
  7. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes to allow the cheese to melt and brown slightly.
  8. Cool and serve: Let the lasagna sit for about 5-10 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and makes it easier to serve.

Nutritional Info (per serving, based on 8 servings):

  • Calories: ~300
  • Protein: ~28g
  • Fat: ~10g
  • Carbs: ~30g
